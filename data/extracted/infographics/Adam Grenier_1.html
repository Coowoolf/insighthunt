Product Strategy

Emerging Channel
Evaluation Framework

with Adam Grenier

A three-part criteria system for deciding when to invest resources into hype-cycle or new marketing channels to avoid wasted effort.

01

Overlap Analysis

Ensure the channel's medium strengths match the specific user need and company goals. Don't force a square peg into a round hole.

02

Channel DNA

Assess the channel's maturity, risk of disappearance, and monetization strategy. Is this platform stable enough to build on?

03

Company DNA

Evaluate internal risk tolerance and ensure a baseline channel is already working before diversifying resources.

04

Monetization Alignment

Gain an advantage by helping the platform prove their own ad models work. Align your success with theirs.
