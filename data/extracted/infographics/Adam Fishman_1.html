The Growth Competency Model

Featuring Adam Fishman
A strategic framework for hiring and evaluating growth practitioners by breaking the role into four quadrants. It prioritizes balancing a team's collective skills over hunting for non-existent "unicorn" individuals.
01

Growth Execution

Focuses on getting things done. Requires channel fluency, rigorous experimentation, and the ability to productize learnings into scalable features.

02

Customer Knowledge

Understanding who you are building for. Involves data fluency, deep insight into user psychology, and applying a creative approach to solve needs.

03

Growth Strategy

The high-level view. Includes loop modeling, efficient capital allocation, and ruthless prioritization to ensure resources target the highest impact.

04

Communication & Influence

How you move the organization. Covers strategic communication, team leadership, and effective stakeholder management.
