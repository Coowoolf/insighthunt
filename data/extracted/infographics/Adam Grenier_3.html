Team Culture

Improv for Cross-Functional Alignment

Methodology by Adam Grenier
Utilizing improvisation techniques to improve communication, reduce defensiveness, and foster collaborative problem-solving within teams.
01

Yes, And

Acknowledge the reality of a partner's statement and build upon it rather than denying it. Validate first, then add value.

02

Gift of Details

Provide specific details in communication to give collaborators more material to work with. Ambiguity kills collaboration.

03

Coexisting Truths

Accept that different teams (e.g., local vs. central) can have conflicting valid goals without one being "wrong". Friction is often structural, not personal.
