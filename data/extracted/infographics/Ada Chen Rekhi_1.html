Curiosity Loops

Methodology by Ada Chen Rekhi
A structured approach to decision-making that involves querying a specific group of trusted individuals with a precise, low-friction question to gather contextual data and identify blind spots.
1
Ask a specific question that solicits rationale. Avoid vague questions like "what should I do?"
2
Curate the audience: include Subject Matter Experts and people who know you personally.
3
Make the ask lightweight to reduce cognitive load and increase response rate.
4
Look for "hard feedback" or surprises rather than validation.
5
Close the loop by thanking participants and sharing the outcome.
