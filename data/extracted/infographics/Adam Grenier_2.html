The Growth CMO Archetype

A leadership model that merges traditional marketing creativity with the analytical and iterative rigor of product management.
01

Agile Marketing

Managing brand and campaigns via sprints and backlogs rather than long-term fixed plans.

02

Total Integration

Product and marketing are 'married at the hip' rather than siloed functions.

03

Data-Driven Branding

Measuring brand and retention with the same rigor as acquisition funnels.

04

Full-Stack Experimentation

Testing across the entire funnel and brand elements, not just acquisition channels.
