Onboarding as a
Retention Lever

Methodology by Adam Fishman
A strategic approach to onboarding that prioritizes long-term habit formation over immediate conversion. This often involves adding friction or 'opinionated defaults' to ensure high-quality users engage correctly.
1

Align brand promise with product delivery immediately.

2

Use "Opinionated Defaults" to make the right choices the easiest choices.

3

Identify high-potential users early (via data or manual review) and provide high-touch intervention.

4

Accept lower conversion rates if it leads to significantly higher cohort retention.
