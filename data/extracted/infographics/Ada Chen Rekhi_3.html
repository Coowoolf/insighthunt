Eating Your Vegetables

Guest: Ada Chen Rekhi
Execution
A heuristic for overcoming the initial resistance to necessary but uncomfortable professional tasks (like networking or writing) by mandating a minimum number of attempts.
01
Commit to 10-12 exposures to a new activity before judging your affinity for it.
02
Distinguish between "I hate this" and "I am bad at this because I am new".
03
Create artificial rules or gamification (e.g., "touch the back wall") to force completion.
04
Reframe the output (e.g., "write for an audience of one") to lower the barrier to entry.
