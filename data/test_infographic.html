Empowered Product Teams
vs. Feature Teams

A framework distinguishing between teams that exist to serve the business by building requested features and teams that exist to solve hard problems in ways their customers love.

01
Teams are assigned problems to solve, not features to build.
02
Success is measured by outcomes (results), not output (shipping code).
03
Decisions are pushed down to the people with the knowledge.
04
Engineers and designers are partners in discovery, not just implementers.
“
“

In a real product team, instead of being given a roadmap of prioritized features, they are given problems to solve.
